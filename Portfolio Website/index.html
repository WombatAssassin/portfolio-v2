About Me

Click us! (or don't, I'm not your mother..)

I am a coder.

I'm a junior developer, with a background in copywriting, based in London, UK.

My road to becoming a junior dev started last year, after discovering an itching curiosity for all things coding. I've had a fantastic time learning, and it's only strengthened my desire to start my career in the industry.

Languages, libraries and frameworks I've worked with: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Typescript, React, Tailwind CSS

My personal philosophy.

At my core, I'm a creative. I detest cookie-cutter designs and cookie-cutter personalities/people.

I believe life is fueled by every-day interactions, and that making someone smile, laugh or pause in awe is one of the most rewarding and noble ways one can spend their time.

UI/UX should be fit for purpose, and leave on people a lasting impression.

My hobbies: coding, writing, playing ukulele, gym.

I am a writer.

I believe that humour and creativity is at the core of every human being. Whether we have these things ourselves or not, we all strive for them in the products and people we interact with.

Much like my personality, I prefer creative, quirky, fun, unique and humourous designs, copy and content to the predictable, the dull, the flat.

If my work isn't at least noteworthy to whoever views it, I'm doing something wrong.
